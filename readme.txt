== Installation ==

1. Goto your WordPress dashboard
2. Ensure WooCommerce is installed and activated
3. Activate Price Bands for WooCommerce
4. Click 'Price Bands' from the left menu
5. Add/edit price bands as required
6. Go to WooCommerce products via the left menu 'Products > All Products' and edit a product
7. For simple products in the 'Product Data > General' section select a price band
8. For variable products in the 'Product Data > Variations > Variation Name' section select a price band
9. Now the price band has been applied the products regular and/or sale price is now set to the price bands pricing
10. Once you have applied your price bands to all products you can easily update pricing across all the products assigned to a brand by editting the price of a band in 'Products > Price Bands'

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Are there any limits to the amount of price bands I can add? =

There are no limits to the amount of price bands you can add.

= If I update a price band will this update prices across all the products assigned to that price band? =

Yes, however please note that this is dependent on how many products you have and the resources of your web hosting platform.

= If I remove a price band from a product what will the products price be? =

If you remove a price band from a product the price will remain the price band price, you can then manually update the price as normal.

= What happens if I try to update a price/sale price when a price band is assigned? =

Upon save the price will revert to the price band's pricing, if you wish to amend the price you should unassign the price band from the product.
